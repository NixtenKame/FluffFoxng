h1. FluffFox Discord Rules

h2. 1. Respect Others

- Treat everyone kindly - no harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or bullying.
- No excessive trolling, personal attacks, or profanity meant to provoke.

h2. 2. Keep It SFW

- All content must be safe for work and friendly for ages 13+.
- Absolutely no NSFW, gore, or offensive imagery/text.

h2. 3. Follow Art Posting Guidelines

- Only share your own art unless you have permission from the original artist.
- Clearly label AI-generated art.
- Post art in the proper channels only.

h2. 4. No Spam or Self-Promo (Without Permission or in Designated Channels)

- No spamming messages, emojis, links, or images.
- Self-promotion is only allowed where staff say it is okay.

h2. 5. Use Channels Properly

- Keep discussions relevant to each channel's topic.
- Off-topic chats belong in general channels.

h2. 6. Respect Moderators & Reporting

- Follow staff instructions respectfully.
- Do not argue with mods; if there is an issue, report it.
- Report rule-breakers instead of engaging in conflict.

h2. 7. No Unapproved Bots or Scripts

- Only use bots approved by staff.
- Do not run unauthorized scripts or automation.

h2. 8. No Drama or Controversial Topics (Except in Designated Channels)

- Avoid politics, religion, or other divisive subjects.
- Resolve conflicts privately or with staff help.

h2. 9. No Impersonation or Misinformation

- Do not pretend to be others or staff.
- No spreading false info or rumors.

h2. Consequences

Breaking rules may lead to warnings, mutes, kicks, or bans at moderators' discretion.
